FOOTBALL BOOTS

Football boots(also called soccer cleats) are special shoes designed to give players grip, control, and comfort when playing on different types of pitches.

They usually have studs on the sole to prevent slipping, help with quick movements, and improve performance.

Modern boots are, made with durable materials, and often include features that enhance ball control and protect the foot.

They come in different stud configurations for:

  • firm ground
  • soft ground
  • artificial turf
  • indoor courts
